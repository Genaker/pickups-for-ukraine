Pickups for Ukraine

Millions of Ukrainian people are digging in to defend their homeland in the freezing cold of winter!
They need specific items to survive in the fight against the aggressor, and many of the European stores are already sold out.
There are also 3M+ refugees who have crossed out of Ukraine into Europe and other neighboring countries who got out with little more than a suitcase.

Donate Pickups and Equipment to Ukraine

How it works:

  • You donate money We buy new and used vehicles both in Ukraine and EU, USA.
  • Our volunteers will deliver the car to Ukraine as soon as possible.
  •  Purchased vehicles will be transferred to mobile paramedics units of the Armed Forces, Territory Defense, National Guard, Medical Service to evacuate wounded soldiers, civil people, deliver different humanitarian aid, food to the Ukrainian towns and villages affected by the Russian Invasion.
Save One Life, Save the Entire World.
 Even 10$ donated dollars can save lives.
